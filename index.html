Space RPG 2

June 2017

Code

  • Angular 1.6
  • Foundation
  • WebPack
  • PixiJS
  • ChartJS
  • MomentJS

A more ambitious version of Space RPG. When you dock at a station you can click 'Services'. There are many places to visit including a casino with a real game of blackjack you can play a stock brokerage where you can buy and sell stocks. I randomly generate the stock prices by creating 3 random trend lines for each stock and layer them on top each other to determine the current price. The higher the trend line layer the more often it has to be regenerated. This keeps the stocks random but still gives them order. I also built a generated galaxy in the map tab that's different for each player using PixiJS. I'm excited to get more into physics and animation. I tried to make everything I could completely procedural.
